Getting started

How Orvyn
works.

From your first word to your Inner Map — here's exactly what happens, and why it's different from anything you've tried before.

Step one
Say what you're carrying

Open Orvyn and type — or hold the mic and speak. There's no right way to begin. "I can't sleep." "I'm scared about tomorrow." "I don't know what I'm feeling." All of it is enough.

💡 The more specific you are, the more precise your session
Step two
Orvyn builds your session

In under 60 seconds, your input becomes a fully guided session — written fresh, voiced specifically for you. Tone, pacing, imagery, language: all shaped around what you brought. Not a template. Not a library pick. Yours.

💡 Sessions are 5–25 minutes depending on your intention
Step three
Listen. Let it in.

Find a quiet spot. Put in headphones if you can. Close your eyes. The session guides you through breath, imagery, and reflection — built around the specific weight you named. Replays are always free.

💡 Even 5 minutes can shift something
Step four
Check in. One tap.

After your session, Orvyn asks how you feel — one tap from five options. Takes five seconds. This single moment is what builds your Inner Map over time.

Step five
Watch your Inner Map grow

After your first week, patterns begin to surface. Orvyn shows you what you return to, what's shifting, what's still heavy. You'll see yourself in a way you've never had words for before.

💡 The map is private. Only you see it.
Ongoing
Orvyn remembers. Sessions deepen.

Every session is stored. Orvyn carries your language, your pace, your patterns forward. The longer you practice, the more precisely it meets you — and the more clearly the map shows your transformation.

The story

Why Orvyn
exists.

Built for the people who are functioning — but not yet healing.

"I was using three different wellness apps and none of them felt like they knew me. They knew their library. They didn't know what I was carrying that Tuesday."

The gap we noticed

Wellness technology had a problem. It was building enormous libraries — thousands of sessions, hundreds of programs — and calling it personalization. But a library is not personalization. It's curation. And no matter how good the curation, it would never know what you were carrying on this specific Tuesday morning at 3am.

Orvyn was built to close that gap. Not to replace meditation. Not to be a shortcut. But to be the first wellness tool that actually listens before it speaks.

What we believe

  • Your inner world deserves more than a playlist.
  • Transformation should be visible, not just felt.
  • Personalization is not a feature — it's the foundation.
  • A wellness tool that doesn't remember you isn't really a tool — it's a vending machine.

The Inner Map

The thing that makes Orvyn different from every other app — including the ones using AI — is the Inner Map. Most apps end when the session ends. Orvyn begins there. Every check-in, every session, every week adds a point to a map that's uniquely yours. Patterns emerge. You see what's lifting and what's still heavy. You watch yourself change.

We believe that seeing your transformation is as important as experiencing it.

Our commitment to you

  • No ads. Ever. Your inner world is not an inventory.
  • No data selling. What you share stays between you and Orvyn.
  • No manipulation. We optimize for your wellbeing, not your screen time.
  • Transparency first. We'll always tell you how your data is used.

Where we're going

Orvyn is early. We have a clear vision and a deep commitment to building something that genuinely helps — not something that looks like it helps. We'd rather have a thousand people whose lives were meaningfully changed than a million who barely opened the app.

If you're here, you're one of the first. We're grateful for that.

What we collect

Orvyn collects only what's needed to provide the service: your account information (email, name), session content you share, mood check-in data for your Inner Map, and basic usage analytics (pages visited, features used).

What we never do

Cookie Policy

Cookies.
Your control.

We use cookies to keep Orvyn running and to understand how it's being used. Here's exactly what each one does — and how to control it.

A cookie is a small file stored in your browser. Orvyn uses them in three categories. You're in control of the optional ones.

Essential cookies

Required for Orvyn to function. These handle your login session, security tokens, and basic site operations. They cannot be disabled.

Analytics cookies

Help us understand how people use Orvyn — which features are used most, where people get stuck, how sessions are discovered. All data is anonymized. No personal content is included.

Preference cookies

Remember your settings across visits — language preference, theme, and notification choices. Optional but improve your experience.

Privacy Policy

What we never use cookies for

  • Advertising or retargeting
  • Selling data to third parties
  • Tracking you across other websites
  • Profiling your wellness data for commercial use
